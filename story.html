OUR STORY

In recent years, venturing on a food business became the leading business concept among other business trends. These successful and well-known food establishments have their own unique style that never goes wrong when it comes to satisfying the consumers’ needs for great food. Today, many Filipinos aspire to come up with a restaurant, food cart, or kiosk businesses. For interested business opportunists, the first question is “Shall I put up my business?” or “Shall I try franchising?” Franchising is the ideal way for those entrepreneurs who don’t want to fuss with the preparation and anxiety of what to sell, how to come up with delectable products, and whether it will hit the masses.

One of the businesses in our industry with promising opportunities is already open for franchising. Lady Christine’s Baby Back Ribs has established a reputable name when it comes to serving scrumptious baby back ribs. The process of achieving the meat’s present quality and arriving with its incomparable sauce attribute to insurmountable moments of experiment and expertise which is why it became well perceived and widely accepted with open arms by the local market. Today, Lady Christine’s Baby Back Ribs also come up with different dishes to warmly serve valuable customers. The ranch style outlet provides a unique and cozy design for diners.

Lady Christine’s Baby Back Ribs is managed and owned by Dr. Joebert Gimeno, a food enthusiast. Dr. Gimeno, fond of cooking and experimenting, came up with starting the business. The success of Lady Christine’s Baby Back Ribs started last 2011 from a take-out counter patronized by Class A and Class B food parishioners in their area. Increased patronage led to many franchise inquiries resulting to the opening of Lady Christine’s Baby Back Ribs outlets at various locations such as Lancaster Square Mall, SM City Lipa, SM City Dasmarinas, SM City Bacoor, SM City Calamba, SM City Molino, SM City Trece Martires, SM City Manila, and a soon to open branch in Fora, Tagaytay. Lady Christine’s Baby Back Ribs aspire to be the country’s leading food chain business that specializes on baby back ribs.

MISSION

  • Ensure quality and affordable Baby Back Ribs and other related products offered

  • Offer family oriented “customer service"

  • Provide exceptional customers service

  • Develop competent and skilled service crew with pleasant personalities

VISION

To be the leading restaurant that offers baby back ribs as its carrier product with amazing taste suited to every people’s palate known by all Filipinos; and to expand business partnership outside the country.
